# Brick Breaker

A classic brick breaker game with black & white design.

## How to Play
- The game opens on a **start menu** — press **SPACE** or **Click** to begin
- **Mouse** or **← →** arrow keys to move paddle
- **SPACE** or **Click** to launch the ball
- **P** to pause

## Play the Game

[Click here to play!](https://fountainnsz-ux.github.io/brick-breaker/brick-breaker.html)

## Features
- Black & white minimalist design
- Particle effects on brick destruction
- Dynamic brick colors based on score
- Ball speed increases per brick hit
- Pause menu
- Sound effects
